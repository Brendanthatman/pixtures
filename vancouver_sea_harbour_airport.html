Vancouver Sea Harbour Airport

rate

I took this image at Vancouver Sea Harbour Airport. I was in town for a convention at the Vancouver Convention Centre, but with an extra day before the convention started to explore the city. When walking to the back side of the convention centre, I find this amazing spot to see the view across the harbour and observe the many aircraft movements that occur here. It's so busy, in fact, that the harbour airport has its own dedicated air traffic control tower, perched on top of a nearby skyscraper.
