Michigan's Adventure: A Ghost Town

rate

On a relatively calm winter day, I took a flight in a Cessna 172. Taking off from Muskegon, Michigan and going north just a little ways, I stumble upon Michigan's Adventure. Growing up in Michigan my whole life, I have been to this place several times. It's pretty cool, with a nice water park and a number of roller coasters and other rides to explore. However, not many people get to see it with nobody in the park. I found it very interesting to be able to see this park from above with no people in it. No water in the pools, no person on the rides.
